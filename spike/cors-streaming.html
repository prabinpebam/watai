Watai — direct browser → Azure OpenAI spike

Verifies the two Phase-1 unknowns from ADR-003: (a) the browser is allowed to call your Azure OpenAI endpoint directly (CORS), and (b) Server-Sent-Events streaming parses incrementally. Everything runs locally — your key is only sent to your own endpoint and is never stored or logged. For a faithful test of the production origin, serve this from the same origin as the app (e.g. copy into public/spike/ and open the Pages URL), or run npx serve spike and open it over http://localhost.
